01 / The Method

01

See it first.

Awareness. Most encounters are won — or avoided entirely — in the seconds before contact. You learn to read intent and posture before hands ever move.

02

Own the space.

Distance. Control the gap and you control the encounter. Footwork and framing do more work than fists, and they keep you in charge of the range.

03

Commit, then recover.

Decision. When it's time to act, act fully — then reset and get out. Trained responses don't freeze, and they don't over-stay.
